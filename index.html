The Method

The
program.

The Ultima Method is a full-spectrum approach designed to build resilient strength, exceptional movement quality, and total-body control.

High-energy, high-intensity strength and conditioning sessions that develop raw strength, muscular endurance, metabolic conditioning, injury resilience, and superior movement quality — all in one cohesive system.

Every class is scalable: lighter options and regressions for beginners, heavier loads and thicker bands for advanced participants. Expect to leave every Ultima session feeling stronger, more stable, deeply connected to your core, and properly challenged.

Minimal equipment. Maximum results. No repetitive routines — just purposeful, progressive training that forges a more capable, balanced you.

Five focused classes  ·  50 minutes each  ·  All levels
01
Full Body Equilibrium
Heavy dumbbell and resistance-band supersets paired with stability and balance training. Total-body integration from the ground up.
02
Lower Body Forge
Deep core activation, unilateral strength, and lower-body power. Built for athletes who demand more from their foundation.
03
Core Ignition
Agility work, deep core sequencing, and anti-rotation training. Coordination and control at the centre of everything.
04
Upper Body Strength
Resistance-band supersets and dumbbell pressing mechanics targeting upper-body strength, posture, and metabolic conditioning.
05
Glutes & Arms Forge
Targeted hypertrophy and muscular endurance for the posterior chain and arms. Precision loading. Purposeful volume.

The Method

The Method is the guiding philosophy and coaching framework at the foundation of Ultima. It is not a programme. It is an operating system — one that structures how training is designed, delivered, and measured at every level.

Why Ultima

Ultima is the global expression of The Method. Application-only. Globally distributed. Uncompromising in its demand for precision.

Where The Method is the science, Ultima is the practice.

The standard

We do not ask members to be their best. We give them a system that makes that the inevitable outcome. Precision coaching. Measured recovery. Zero ambiguity.

Hong Kong

Ultima is headquartered in Hong Kong — a city built on discipline, precision, and the relentless pursuit of the exceptional. The network is deliberately small. That is not a limitation. That is the point.
